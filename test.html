What is
ING Chances
for Children?
ING Chances for Children is a programme run by the ING Group in collaboration with UNICEF to guarantee children's right to education.
We have worked in countries including India, Zambia, Ethiopia and Brazil, to help provide schooling for more than 783,000 children by supplying educational material, building schools and training teachers.
But there is still a long way to go. There are still 57 million children who do not go to school or who receive sporadic or insufficient education.
